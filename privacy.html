Privacy

Privacy Policy

This page explains how this static V1 website handles visitor information. It should be reviewed before final public launch.

Last updated: May 28, 2026

Summary

This site does not have accounts, contact forms, payments, donations, analytics, or its own backend. The page exists to present public information about Diez42 and direct visitors to confirmed contact channels.

Information we store

The site stores your language and theme preferences in your browser's local storage. That information stays on your device and is used only to remember your selection on future visits.

Hosting and technical logs

The site is published through GitHub Pages. Like any web hosting service, GitHub may process technical data such as IP address, browser type, requested page, and access time to deliver the site and protect the service.

External links

Links to WhatsApp, Instagram, and Facebook open external services. If you choose to use them, those services may process your data under their own privacy policies.

Images and draft content

Current program images are selected Diez42 photos pending final web-use and consent confirmation. Site copy is V1 draft content and should be confirmed before final launch.

Contact

For privacy questions or to contact Diez42, use the published channels on the homepage: WhatsApp, phone, or the listed mailing address.
